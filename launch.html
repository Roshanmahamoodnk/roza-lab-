Launch

Use this page to plan opening day, location fit, content strategy, and the first menu edits after launch.

Location fit

Koramangala, Indiranagar, HSR, Whitefield, Bellandur, Sarjapur Road.

Target crowd

IT, business, couples, social groups, and night dessert buyers.

Launch format

Small premium menu, strong visuals, tasting flights, and late-night service.

Opening KPIs

What to track

  1. Average order value
  2. Scoop mix versus sundae mix
  3. Best-selling flavor by time of day
  4. Wastage and hold time
  5. Repeat visits and social engagement

Launch sequence

First 30 days

  1. Soft launch with 8 hero flavors.
  2. Collect feedback on sweetness, aroma, and texture.
  3. Promote flights and date-night sundaes.
  4. Retain winners and remove weak flavors.
  5. Lock the final signature menu after testing.
