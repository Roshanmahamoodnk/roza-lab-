Kitchen discipline

craft

quality in every step

The product only feels premium when the process is precise: mix balance, pasteurization, aging, churn temperature, storage, and service temperature.

Pasteurize82–85°C for 15–20 sec

Production flow

a calm, repeatable SOP

  1. 01Sanitize all tools and verify batch weights.
  2. 02Dry blend sugar, milk powder, dextrose, and stabilizer.
  3. 03Heat milk and cream, then pasteurize and cool fast.
  4. 04Age the mix, add flavor, churn, and pack.
  5. 05Blast freeze, harden, label, and store correctly.
"If the process is disciplined, the flavor can stay elegant instead of busy."

Texture control

dense, clean, and polished

Keep the overrun modest, the fat balance stable, and the inclusions purposeful. The goal is a finish that feels expensive rather than airy or artificial.

Good service is also part of craft: clear scoop temperature, strong visual merchandising, and a counter setup that looks orderly, not cluttered.
