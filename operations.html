Production flow

A calm SOP that can be repeated.

This is the path from raw ingredients to a finished scoop. The goal is not speed alone; the goal is stable texture, safe handling, and a clean premium finish.

01 Sanitise and weigh Clean the bench, confirm weights, and keep every input traceable. start
02 Dry blend Combine sugar, milk powder, stabiliser, dextrose, and salt before heating. base
03 Heat and pasteurise Reach 82–85°C and cool quickly so texture and safety stay in control. control
04 Age and flavour Let the mix rest at 4°C, then add the flavour system and churn. develop
05 Harden and store Blast freeze, label, and hold the product at the right temperature. finish

Critical control points

Where the product can drift.

The point of the SOP is not paperwork. It is to keep the final flavour, texture, and safety exactly where the studio expects them to be.

  • Record heating and cooling temperatures every batch.
  • Keep sanitation and weighing as the first non-negotiable step.
  • Watch holding time, freezer load, and service temperature.
  • Check garnish, portion size, and finish before the product reaches the guest.
