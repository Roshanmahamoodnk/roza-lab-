Core SOP

Production flow

  1. Sanitize station and verify temperatures.
  2. Dry blend sugar, milk powder, dextrose, and stabilizer.
  3. Heat milk and cream, pasteurize, and cool quickly.
  4. Age the mix, flavor it, churn, pack, and harden.
  5. Store at -18°C or below and serve with correct scoop temperature.

Texture and temperature

What makes the product feel premium.

Overrun

Keep it modest for a dense, high-end mouthfeel.

Serving temp

Scoop around -12 to -15°C for ideal softness.

Hardened storage

Maintain -18°C or lower for stable texture.

Inclusions

Add crunch at the end to protect texture contrast.
