Operations

This page covers production flow, equipment, staffing, and quality control so the premium menu can run consistently every day.

Core SOP

Production flow

  1. Sanitize station and verify temperatures.
  2. Dry blend sugar, milk powder, dextrose, and stabilizer.
  3. Heat milk and cream, pasteurize, and cool quickly.
  4. Age the mix, flavor it, churn, pack, and harden.
  5. Store at -18°C or below and serve with correct scoop temperature.
