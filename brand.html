How the brand should feel

premium, calm, engineered.

The site should present Rozalab as a research-led ice cream project with enough luxury to feel premium and enough structure to feel credible.

The best pages show the pairing logic, the sample development path, and the system needed to move from prototype to production.

Visual system

Editorial, not templated.

Typography

Oversized headlines with clear hierarchy and short supporting copy.

Palette

Warm white, espresso, blush, honey, and soft mint accents.

Motion

Subtle reveal, gentle drift, and useful interaction instead of noise.

Story

Show the why behind the product: pairing, process, and scale.

Client value

Pairing clarity

Scale story

How the concept moves from research to manufacturing and QSR rollout.
