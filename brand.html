Since 2026 in Bangalore

Made for the city’s modern dessert ritual.

The brand sits between boutique gelateria, craft cafe, and science-led dessert bar. The goal is not volume. The goal is a place where one scoop feels considered.

That means careful naming, restrained visuals, short flavor copy, and a website that feels closer to an editorial feature than a sales page.

01 Tradition Respect the discipline of gelato making: small batches, temperature control, and a clean finish.
02 Craftsmanship Use simple compositions and let ingredients do the work rather than piling on decorations.
03 Discovery Keep a premium menu with a few surprising pairings that feel modern but still familiar.

Visual system

the site should breathe

Big type, long margins, and thin borders keep the presentation closer to the references you shared: image-led, text-led, and not overloaded with effects.

Typography

Use oversized headings and short supporting copy.

Palette

Warm white, espresso, blush, pale gold, and olive mint.

Spacing

Let each section feel like a page in a magazine spread.

Photography
