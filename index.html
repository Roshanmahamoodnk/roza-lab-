Process, SOP, CCP

How the ice cream is made without losing quality.

01 Sanitise and weigh Clean tools, verify batch weights, and keep all inputs traceable.
02 Blend the dry base Milk powder, sugar, dextrose, stabilizer, and salt get combined first.
03 Heat, pasteurize, cool Hit 82–85°C, then cool quickly to protect texture and safety.
04 Age, flavor, churn Let the mix mature at 4°C, then add the flavor system and churn.
05 Harden, label, store Blast freeze, label, hold at the right temperature, and serve correctly.
  • CCP focus: temperature logging, sanitation, holding time, and mix integrity.
  • Service focus: texture, scoop temperature, garnish discipline, and visual merchandising.
  • Manufacturing focus: repeatability, batch traceability, and process stability.
