Scale path

From sample development to a real launch system.

The site should make the growth route obvious: first a stable sample, then a pilot batch, then a handoff to manufacturing or QSR once the product is proven.

1. Sample development

Test flavour balance, texture, and the visual story before any public promise is made.

2. Pilot production

Keep the recipe stable, document the controls, and make the results reproducible.

3. Manufacturing handoff

Move stable SKUs into a partner environment without losing flavour quality.

4. QSR growth

Keep the story premium while making the service model fast and reliable.
