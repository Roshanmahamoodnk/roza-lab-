Evidence at a glance

What makes the project credible.

Roza Lab is built from measurable inputs: public market signals, ingredient pairing scores, controlled process temperatures, and a tight menu strategy.

Market data

Public Bangalore listings point to ₹150–200, ₹300–400, and ₹600–700 spend bands for two people.

Pairing evidence

Lychee + rosewater scored 0.3197 and mango + passion fruit scored 0.3523 — both very high.

Process control

Pasteurize at 82–85°C, age at 4°C, churn at -5 to -7°C, serve at -12 to -15°C.

Menu discipline

Black sesame + vanilla scored low, so the menu stays curated instead of crowded.
