Process & SOP

The same five steps,every single batch.

STEP 01

Sanitise & weigh

Every contact surface sanitised; every input weighed to the gram and logged before anything is blended.

STEP 02

Blend the dry base

Milk powder, sugar, dextrose, stabiliser and salt combined dry so they hydrate evenly, batch to batch.

STEP 03

Pasteurise & cool 82–85 °C

The mix is held in the pasteurisation window, then cooled — the step that decides both safety and mouthfeel.

STEP 04

Age, flavour & churn

The base ages to develop body; flavours and inclusions go in and the mix churns to target overrun.

STEP 05

Harden, label & store

Blast-hardened, labelled with batch and date, moved to cold storage with the chain logged end to end.

Critical Control Points — logged, not assumed
Temperature

Pasteurisation & storage logged per batch.

Sanitation

Contact surfaces verified before each run.

Holding time

Age and hold windows tracked to spec.

Mix integrity

Overrun & consistency checked pre-hardening.
