Formulation

Master base, process controls, and product logic.

Use one disciplined base and vary flavor systems around it. That keeps texture consistent while allowing the menu to feel premium and diverse.

Master dairy base per 1 kg mix

  • Milk580 g
  • Cream150 g
  • Sugar140 g
  • Dextrose / glucose40 g
  • Skim milk powder55 g
  • Stabilizer / emulsifier3 g
  • Salt1 g

Process controls

  • Pasteurize82–85°C for 15–20 sec
  • Age the mix4°C for 4–12 hours
  • Churn draw temp-5 to -7°C
  • Harden-18 to -25°C
  • Serve-12 to -15°C
